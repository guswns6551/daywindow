About DayWindow

DayWindow is a lightweight tool to help you estimate your Schengen stay under the 90/180 rule. Enter your trip dates and DayWindow shows:

  • Days used in the last 180 days
  • Days remaining out of 90
  • Overstay risk status
  • Next possible entry date (if you are over 90)
